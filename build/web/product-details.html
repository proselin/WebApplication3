History of coco chanel noir

Chanel Coco Noir EDP was released by the inventor Jacques Polge in 2012. Coco Noir is like a lyrical Baroque accompaniment or another journey of Gabrielle who is looking for memories left under the sky. mysterious night in beautiful Venice.

Jacques Polge, the father of the Coco Noir fragrance shared: "With Coco Noir, I thought of Coco and Coco Mademoiselle fragrances because they are an integral part of the past. I want to continue to explore the world of perfumery. Chanel perfume, a very different and different world from the usual floral lines of Bois-des-Iles and Cuir de Russie. And I started the idea with Coco. Like the culture called CHANEL.
